About

I'm Igor Zavalyshyn. I'm a post-doc at UCLouvain university in Louvain-la-Neuve, Belgium (not to be confused with UCL in London nor KU Leuven in, well, Leuven). I have a PhD degree in Distributed Computing from Instituto Superior Técnico in Portugal and UCLouvain in Belgium.

During my PhD under the supervision of my supervisors Nuno Santos and Ramin Sadre I worked on privacy issues of IoT, specifically within the smart home environment, and built private-by-design IoT systems that span across local home, cloud and mobile environments. The code of all of these systems is open and publicly available at my Github page. You can find a full list of my publications at the following DBLP page.

I read, surf and do bouldering whenever I have free time.
